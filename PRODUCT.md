# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Tawny-SOC is for operators and detection engineers who want a small, self-hosted AI SOC surface beside the Tawny EDR. They need fast alert triage, detection rule review, playbook execution, and clean proof that endpoint logs are landing in a SIEM-like workspace.

## Product Purpose

Tawny-SOC complements Tawny by acting as a SOC/SIEM log sink and investigation console. It receives Tawny alerts and telemetry over HTTP, stores them locally for development, maps them to Sigma-style rules and ATT&CK techniques, and gives analysts a simple closed-loop workflow for triage, investigation, containment planning, and detection improvement.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, code-literate, and direct. The product should feel like a trustworthy analyst console, not a theatrical cyber dashboard.

## Anti-references

Avoid neon threat maps, fake global attack arcs, oversized marketing metrics, glass panels, and decorative AI copy. Avoid hiding the detection logic behind vague automation.

## Design Principles

1. Put live operational state first: ingestion, open alerts, noisy rules, and current cases.
2. Make every AI recommendation auditable: show evidence, rule matches, confidence, and next actions.
3. Treat detections as code: rule source, ATT&CK tags, tests, and tuning notes should be visible.
4. Keep workflows explicit: analysts should understand what each playbook will do before running it.
5. Preserve Tawny’s restrained product feel while giving the SOC enough density for repeated analyst use.
